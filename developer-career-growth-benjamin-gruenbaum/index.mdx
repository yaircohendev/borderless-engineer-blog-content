Our guest for the episode is Benjamin Gruenbaum.

Benjamin is a Developer at Microsoft and a core team member of Node.js, Bluebird and Mobx.

We talked about:

- Should experienced developers get into open source contributions?
- What are the benefits and different tradeoffs?
- For those who want to get started with open source, how to do so.
- StackOverflow - should you still answer questions these days?
- Recommended books for developers
- Development Culture
- Tech Leadership  
    And more
